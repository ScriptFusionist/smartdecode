Help & Guide

I made SmartDecode because I was tired of trying to download comics or novels and ending up on sites full of ads, popups, or shady redirects. This tool helps you instantly uncover the real link hidden behind encoding like base64, URL, hex, or shortlinks.

Supported formats:

  • Base64 (e.g. aHR0cHM6Ly9leGFtcGxlLmNvbQ==)
  • URL-encoded (e.g. https%3A%2F%2Fexample.com)
  • Hexadecimal (e.g. 68747470733a2f2f...)
  • Some shortlink services like adtival, etc.

Decoded history is saved locally in your browser. You can view it from the History button. All your data stays private.

Note: This tool does not bypass ads or captchas. It only extracts the clean, hidden link.
